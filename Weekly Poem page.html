Poem of the Week

The Dreamer's Journey

In the twilight's gentle embrace,
Where dreams and reality interlace,
A wanderer treads the moonlit way,
On a journey that colors the night grey.

With every step, worlds unfurl,
Stories whispered, secrets hurl,
The dreamer roams through time and space,
Seeking solace, finding grace.

The stars ignite, a celestial guide,
Through galaxies unknown, side by side,
The dreamer dances, weightless and free,
Weaving tapestries of possibility.

- Emily Rivers
