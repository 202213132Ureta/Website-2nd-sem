Drugs

Daniel Ureta


Light as a feather, she felt
As she stray closer to her death,
She's off in her little world
Unable to say a word.

Her breathes gets slower and slower,
And the room gets colder and colder,
She suddenly said "I will never be sober"
As the drugs destroy her over and over.

Pointless

Daniel Ureta


All this running around
Trying to cover my shadow
Being different and proud
Always feels so shallow


All this shutting things out
I don't want to be part of it
These problems, These doubts
I can never omit

All these words that I say
Echoes to the void
Lost in the endless fray
I am forever paranoid

Sierra Madre

Benedict D'Bayan


Dakilang Sierra Madre,
Walang nakakapantay sa iyong kariktan!

Nang ikaw ay mabuo,
Pugad ng kagandahan,

At ng lahat ng iba't-ibang
Katangiang kabigha-bighani,
Dumadaloy sa kahabaan ng 'yong kaluluwa.

Dakilang Sierra Madre,
Ang tibay ng 'yong dibdib,
Sa mga naglalakasang bagyo,
Kahinaan ang 'yong dinudulot.
Mga bagay na may buhay at walang buhay, Sa iyong likod ay walang tinatamong sugat,
Gaya nalamang ng poetikong may akda.

Dakilang Sierra Madre,
Ang kapunuan ng iba't-ibang kagandahan sa iyong loob,
Ay maihahalintulad sa Samu't-saring klase ng buhay na namamahay sa dakilang Kabundukan,
Patunay ng buhay na umaagos mula sa 'yong pag iral.

Dakilang Sierra Madre,
Habang ako'y nanonood mula sa siyudad,
Na puno ng karaniwang tanawin,

Ang kagustuhang kong mamuhay at manatili,
Sa iyong mundong puno ng kagandahan,
Lalo pang nag aalab.

Kung ito man ay mangyayari lang sa isang imposibleng panaginip,

Pakiusap, wag niyo na 'kong gisingin.

Rage

Daniel Ureta


Manic in the skies
There's rage in my eyes
Even though it's down below
There's nowhere to go


You all better hide
Take your ass inside
It's burning in my core
And nobody knows

Now I'm down to fight
Killing everybody on sight
Slaying everybody I loathe
Using every bullet in the load

You can run, You can hide
There's no place I can't find
You better know where to go
Cause if I find you I'm gonna put a bullet through your skull.

Manila

Daniel Ureta


I feel the cold wind across my face
As I walk through the streets of my city
Poverty runs deep in my place
But somehow, I am unable to feel pity


The sunlight shines brightly like rhinestones in the sky
With it are the hopes of my people
Knowing that they haven't died
But soon they realize that their hopes are feeble

Hope is a lie we refuse to omit
But we ignore because that hope is what keeps us alive
Even if it's false we still believe it
Because hope is what makes us survive

Colors

Daniel Ureta


The colors that exist,
Brings darkness to me,
The colors that I can't resist,
Are the ones I can't get for free,


The sounds of the colors that I hear,
The happiness that sounds so real,
These things that i can never revere,
And only brings me tears.

Colors that are so honest,
Shades that are so modest,
I can always look past what's violent,
But can never focus on what's prominent.

The colors that exist,
Brings darkness to me
The colors that are free,
Are the ones I will never see,
